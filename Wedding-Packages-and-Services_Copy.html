Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 6-9 months in advance for wedding photography, especially for peak season dates (May-October). Popular dates can book up to a year in advance.

What is your payment policy?

We require a 30% non-refundable retainer to secure your date, with the remaining balance due 30 days before your wedding date. We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal.

How long until we receive our photos?

You'll receive a sneak peek of 15-20 images within 1 week after your wedding. The complete gallery will be delivered within 6-8 weeks during peak season, and 4-6 weeks during off-peak times.

Do you travel for weddings?

Yes! We love destination weddings. Travel within 50 miles is included in our packages. For destinations beyond that, additional travel fees apply. Please contact us for a custom quote.

Have Questions?

Contact us for custom packages or special requests. We're happy to create a tailored experience for your special day.
